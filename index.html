Full groom

Our signature service — bath, blow-dry, breed-specific haircut, nail trim, ear cleaning, and a finishing spritz. Your pup leaves looking and smelling amazing.

Bath & brush

Full shampoo, blow-dry, thorough brushing, ear cleaning, and nail trim — without the haircut. Perfect for short-coated or between-groom maintenance.
